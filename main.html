About Me

I am Samuel, a second-year student in Nee Ann Polytechnic's School of ICT. I am currently pursuing a diploma in Information Technology. I hope to have a career as a game programmer so this portfolio will showcase my work, both school and personal projects.

My History with Games

I grew up with games, from the Wii Sports console to the newest installment in Civilisation. I grew uup playing Wii Sports with my siblings, and playing Mario Kart and Mario Party wiith my cousins on the Nintendo DS. Soon, I moved to mobile games, playing minecraft and Plants vs Zombies on a small iPhone screen. Right before secondary school, I discovered a first-person shooter(FPS) called Team Fortress 2, and I have been gaming on my computer ever since. I have played close to 40 games since I started. Music games like Osu! and Muse Dash, Adventure Games like Trove and Genshin Impact, Sandbox games like ARK Survival and Grand Theft Auto V, FPSs like Valorant and Counter-Strike Global Offensive. The list keeps going on and on, and I hope to try out any new games and explore the many genres and themes games have to offer.

Why Game Production

Ever since I finished O levels, I always wanted to make games. I wanted to make games for peopple to enjoy after a long day of work, or to have fun with their family and friends. After I made my first simple game, it spurred me to learn more as I now understood the creative process that goes behind programming games. Joining this elective was a way for me to understand the industry better as well, as I did not understand how a team of game developers could function together, or how business was done behind the scenes. All in all, I joined GPN to better understand game development and prepare myself for the gaming industry.
